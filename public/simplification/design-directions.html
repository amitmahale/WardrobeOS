Recommendation: Apple Health structure, Airbnb surface, Robinhood moments.

Use Apple Health as the product model: a calm daily summary with useful rings, cards, and trends. Use Airbnb for the visual closet cards and homepage warmth. Use Robinhood only inside high-conviction decision panels like fit score, buy-next unlocks, and “what changed if I add this?” This avoids making the whole app feel like trading, while preserving Robinhood’s best quality: fast action clarity.
